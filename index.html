About Us

When it comes to industry-leading innovations in cycles and accessories, Vicious Cycle products are what you have been waiting for. Vicious Cycles Inc. is an Oxford-based cycle workshop founded in late 2010. Our team of mechanics build the bikes and man the workshop, while our trained customer service staff attends the day-to-day operations. Vicious Cycles is more than just a business. We’re a family of biking enthusiasts.

Your bike is more than just a ride. Every bike should be an extension of its owner, uniquely designed to fulfill your dreams. Biking is more than just transportation, it’s a lifestyle. Here at Vicious Cycles, we bike hard, and we bike often. Don’t just be part of the numbers. Ride your cycle, pursue your dreams, live your life.
